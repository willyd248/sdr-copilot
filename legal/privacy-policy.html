Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

SDR Copilot is a Chrome extension that assists sales development representatives by capturing tab audio during calls, transcribing conversations in real time, and integrating with Gmail and Salesforce to streamline prospecting and CRM workflows.

Because SDR Copilot handles sensitive data including audio, email, and CRM records, this Privacy Policy is detailed. Please read it fully before installing and using the extension.

By installing and using SDR Copilot, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, do not install the extension.

Data We Collect and Process

CategoryWhat we processHow it's handled
Tab audio Audio from the active browser tab (e.g., a sales call in a web conferencing tab) Captured via Chrome's tab capture API and streamed in real time to Deepgram for transcription. Audio is not stored on our servers after the session ends.
Call transcripts Text transcription of call audio generated by Deepgram Stored securely and associated with your account for post-call review and CRM logging

Usage analytics Feature usage, session counts, error events (anonymized) Used to improve the product
Audio capture disclosure: SDR Copilot captures audio from browser tabs when you explicitly activate recording. You are responsible for ensuring that all parties on a call have been informed and have consented to recording, as required by applicable wiretapping and call recording laws in your jurisdiction. SDR Copilot does not activate recording without your explicit action.

Audio Data and Transcription

When you start a recording session, audio from the active tab is streamed in real time to Deepgram for speech-to-text transcription. The following applies:

  • Audio streams are sent over encrypted connections (TLS)
  • Deepgram processes audio to generate transcripts and does not use API audio for model training by default
  • Raw audio is not stored on Deepgram's servers after the transcription session ends (subject to Deepgram's current data retention policy)
  • Transcripts are stored in your SDR Copilot account and are accessible only to you and administrators of your organization account (if applicable)
  • You can delete transcripts from your account at any time

See Deepgram's privacy policy at deepgram.com/privacy.

Gmail API Access

SDR Copilot requests access to your Gmail account to surface email history with prospects and assist with follow-up drafting. Our use of data obtained from Gmail APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We access only the threads and messages necessary to provide the feature you are actively using
  • We do not use Gmail data for advertising or to build profiles for any purpose other than operating the extension
  • We do not share Gmail data with third parties except Salesforce (when you log an email to CRM) and as described in this policy
  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google Account settings

Salesforce API Access

SDR Copilot connects to Salesforce to read prospect and account context and to write call logs and activities when you instruct it to. We access only the objects and records necessary for these functions. We do not read or write Salesforce data without your explicit instruction.

Salesforce data transits our backend solely for the purpose of presenting it in the extension UI or writing it back to Salesforce. We do not independently store or analyze your Salesforce CRM records.

Chrome Extension Permissions

SDR Copilot requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • tabCapture: To capture audio from the active tab when you start a recording
  • tabs: To identify the active tab and access its URL for context
  • storage: To persist settings and session state locally
  • identity: To handle OAuth authentication with Google and Salesforce

The extension does not use permissions beyond what is listed above. It does not access camera, clipboard, or browser history.

Third-Party Services

  • Deepgram: Real-time speech transcription. Audio is streamed during active recording sessions. See deepgram.com/privacy.
  • Google (Gmail API + OAuth): Email access and authentication. See policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Salesforce: CRM data access. Subject to your organization's Salesforce data processing agreements. See salesforce.com/company/privacy.

Data Security

SDR Copilot takes the following security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over TLS-encrypted connections
  • API credentials and OAuth tokens are stored encrypted
  • Access to stored transcripts is restricted to the authenticated account holder
  • We do not log full audio or full email content in server-side application logs

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your account data and stored transcripts
  • Deletion: Delete individual transcripts within the extension, or request full account deletion
  • Revoke integrations: Revoke Gmail access via Google Account settings; revoke Salesforce access via Salesforce Connected Apps
  • Portability: Request an export of your transcripts in plain text or JSON format

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@sdrcopilot.com.

Call Recording Compliance

Call recording laws vary by jurisdiction. In some U.S. states and many countries, all-party consent is required before recording a phone or online call. You are solely responsible for complying with applicable laws when using SDR Copilot's recording features. SDR Copilot is not responsible for your failure to obtain required consents.

Best practice: Always inform all call participants that the call is being recorded, regardless of jurisdiction. Many organizations require this as a standard practice.

Data Retention

Transcripts are retained until you delete them or close your account. Upon account deletion, all transcripts and account data are deleted within 30 days. OAuth tokens are revoked immediately upon account deletion or revocation request.

Children's Privacy

SDR Copilot is a professional tool intended for use by adults in sales roles. We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via the extension update notes and email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

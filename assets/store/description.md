## Short description (132 chars max)
AI-powered sales assistant: live call transcription, real-time objection coaching, and automated follow-ups for SDRs.

## Full description

**SDR Copilot** turns every sales call into a data-driven coaching session — right inside your browser.

### What it does

**Live Transcription**
Deepgram's Nova-2 model transcribes your call in real time. Every word appears in a clean, scrollable overlay while you talk.

**Real-Time Objection Detection**
Six objection categories (pricing, timing, competitor, authority, need, interest) are detected automatically. Coaching suggestions surface at the moment they're needed.

**Automated Post-Call Workflow**
When the call ends, SDR Copilot:
- Drafts a personalized follow-up email in Gmail
- Logs a Task in Salesforce with duration, outcome, and notes
- Records the call to your dashboard for review

**Call Dashboard**
Review every call: duration, objections hit, follow-up actions taken, talk-time split. Export to CSV with one click.

**Demo Mode**
No accounts required to try it. Hit "Watch Demo" from the popup to see a full simulated call with transcript replay, objection detection, and post-call automation.

### Who it's for
Sales Development Representatives who use Orum for dialing and want to spend less time on admin and more time selling.

### Privacy
SDR Copilot processes audio locally in your browser. Transcription is sent to Deepgram's API (your own API key). No call audio is stored by the extension. See our privacy policy for full details.

What is SDR Copilot?

AI coaching for every cold call

SDR Copilot is a free Chrome extension that sits inside Orum while you dial. The moment a prospect raises a pricing concern, mentions a competitor, or starts to disengage, a coaching cue appears on your screen — no typing, no context switching.

After the call, it automatically creates a Salesforce task with the full summary, duration, and disposition, then drafts a personalized follow-up email from your transcript — ready to send in one click.

How It Works

From first ring to logged CRM task

SDR Copilot runs in the background — you just make calls.

1
Open Orum and start dialing
SDR Copilot detects your active Orum tab automatically. The overlay appears when a call connects — no buttons to click, no setup per call.
2
Live transcript streams in real time
Your call audio is streamed to Deepgram Nova-2 for real-time speech-to-text. Both speaker turns — SDR and Prospect — are labeled and shown as the words are spoken.
3
Objection detected → coaching tip appears
When an objection phrase is detected (pricing, competition, timing, authority, interest, or email-me), an instant coaching cue appears in the overlay with a suggested response you can use right now.
4
Call ends → post-call summary generated
A summary screen shows call duration, objection count, talk-time ratio, and suggested follow-up items — all extracted automatically from the transcript.
5
Salesforce task logged, follow-up email drafted
With one click, a Salesforce Task is created with the full summary and disposition. A personalized Gmail draft is ready to review and send — no copy-pasting required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

What is SDR Copilot?
SDR Copilot is a free Chrome extension that provides real-time AI coaching during cold calls on Orum. It detects objections as they happen, suggests responses, transcribes the call, and automatically logs the activity to Salesforce.
How does real-time call coaching work?
SDR Copilot captures your call audio directly in the browser using the Chrome tab capture API, streams it to Deepgram for live transcription, and then analyzes the transcript for objection patterns. When an objection is detected, a coaching cue appears on screen instantly — no manual input required.
Does SDR Copilot work with Salesforce?
Yes. SDR Copilot automatically creates a Salesforce Task after each call with the transcript summary, duration, objections detected, and disposition — no copy-pasting required.
Is SDR Copilot free?
Yes, SDR Copilot is free to install from the Chrome Web Store. You need your own Deepgram API key (free tier available) for transcription.
What sales platforms does SDR Copilot support?
SDR Copilot currently integrates with Orum for call detection and dialing, Salesforce for CRM logging, and Gmail for post-call email drafts.
What objections can SDR Copilot detect?
SDR Copilot detects six objection categories in real time: pricing objections, timing objections, competitor mentions, gatekeeper deflections, "not interested" signals, and requests to email instead of call.
Is my call data private?
Call audio is streamed to Deepgram for transcription over an encrypted connection and is not stored after the session. Transcripts are stored locally in Chrome storage and are never shared with third parties beyond the integrations you enable (Salesforce, Gmail). See the Privacy Policy for full details.
